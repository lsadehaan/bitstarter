Location, location, location

Current real-estate listing sites doesn't make it very easy to find your perfect location. The extent of filtering on location normal goes no further than entering an address or ZIP code. MiCasa.net will change all this with advanced location filtering tools. Use a free-hand drawing tool to select arbitrary areas you are interested in. Filter and rank listings by their distance from public infrastructure such as the metro/schools/university/library/etc in KM/Miles or minutes by foot/car/metro. Filter or rank based on distance from specific points of interest added by you, such as your office or parents home.

Selling or letting?

Easily add new listings through our mobile app. Take pictures using your mobile phone and instantly add to the listing from your phone including GPS coordinates of the location. Built in messaging interface makes it easy for potential clients to contact you live or offline. Certain important information is required, such as property and home square foot. Many additional features will make MiCasa the first location everybody will start to look for their future home.

FAQ

Why use MiCasa.net?
Because it is the easiest way to find the home you've always wanted without having to search through thousands and thousands of listings on hundreds of sites.
Why would Estate Agents want to use MiCasa.net?
Estate Agents will be able to register for an Agent portal. Depending of their subscription they will be able to customize their portal with their own Domain name, artwork and copy and visitors will see only their listings on their portal site. Their listings will still appear on MiCasa.net but once a potential customer clicks on one of their listings they will be redirected to the Agents portal. This features of MiCasa.net will not only benefit people looking for homes but also the Estate agents listing homes.
